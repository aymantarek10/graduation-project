How to Rent a Car

Renting a car through Al Musafir is easy and fast. Follow these steps:

  • Choose pick-up and drop-off locations with suitable dates and times
  • Browse available cars and compare prices and specifications
  • Select the car that suits your needs
  • Complete booking details and enter payment information
  • Receive booking confirmation via email
  • Pick up your car at the scheduled time

Car Rental Requirements

For the best rental experience, please ensure the following requirements are met:

  • Minimum driver age is 21 years (may vary depending on car type)
  • Valid driver's license for at least one year
  • Credit card in the main driver's name
  • Valid passport or national ID
  • For foreign nationals: International driving license or translated local license

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking free of charge 24 hours before pick-up time. Late cancellations may incur cancellation fees.

Is insurance included in the price?

Yes, all prices include basic insurance. You can add additional insurance coverage when booking.

Can I return the car to a different location?

Yes, we offer one-way rental service for an additional fee that varies depending on the distance.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and Mada cards.
