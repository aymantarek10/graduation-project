Iconic Luxury Hotels

5-Star Luxury

Hotel Hassler Roma

Perched atop the Spanish Steps, this legendary hotel has welcomed royalty and celebrities since 1893. Experience unparalleled elegance with breathtaking views of the Eternal City.

Garden Oasis

Hotel de Russie

A Rocco Forte hotel nestled between Piazza del Popolo and Spanish Steps, featuring stunning terraced gardens and world-class spa facilities.

Hilltop Retreat

Rome Cavalieri, A Waldorf Astoria Hotel

Set on a private hilltop with panoramic city views, featuring Michelin-starred dining, an impressive art collection, and expansive Mediterranean gardens.

Rooftop Elegance

Hotel Eden

A Dorchester Collection masterpiece near Villa Borghese with a renowned rooftop restaurant offering spectacular 360-degree views of Rome's skyline.

Boutique Charm

Palazzo Dama

A 19th-century palazzo transformed into a stylish boutique hotel with lush gardens, contemporary design, and authentic Roman atmosphere.

Timeless Grandeur

The St. Regis Rome

Built in 1894, this Belle Époque landmark on Via Vittorio Veneto offers bespoke butler service, opulent suites, and legendary Italian hospitality.

Must-Visit Attractions

Ancient Wonder

The Colosseum

The largest ancient amphitheater ever built, standing since 80 AD. This iconic symbol of Imperial Rome once held 50,000 spectators for gladiatorial contests and public spectacles.

Baroque Masterpiece

Trevi Fountain

Rome's largest and most famous fountain, completed in 1762. Toss a coin over your shoulder to ensure your return to the Eternal City, as legend promises.

Spiritual Heart

Vatican City & St. Peter's Basilica

The world's smallest independent state and center of Catholicism. Marvel at Michelangelo's Sistine Chapel ceiling and explore the magnificent Vatican Museums.

Architectural Marvel

The Pantheon

Best-preserved ancient Roman building, completed in 125 AD. Its massive unreinforced concrete dome remains the world's largest and features an open oculus to the sky.

Ancient Heart

Roman Forum

The center of ancient Roman public life for centuries. Walk among ruins of government buildings, temples, and monuments that shaped Western civilization.

Iconic Landmark

Spanish Steps

The famous 135-step monumental stairway connecting Piazza di Spagna with Trinità dei Monti church. A beloved meeting place and photo spot since 1725.

Baroque Square

Piazza Navona

Built on the site of an ancient Roman stadium, this stunning baroque piazza features Bernini's Fountain of the Four Rivers and vibrant street life.

Art & Gardens

Villa Borghese Gardens

Rome's third-largest park featuring stunning gardens, museums, and galleries. Home to the Borghese Gallery with masterpieces by Bernini, Caravaggio, and Raphael.

Charming District

Trastevere

Rome's most bohemian neighborhood with narrow cobblestone streets, medieval churches, authentic trattorias, and vibrant nightlife along the Tiber River.

World-Class Dining

3 MICHELIN Stars

La Pergola

Rome's only three-Michelin-starred restaurant led by Chef Heinz Beck. Perched atop Rome Cavalieri with breathtaking city views and exceptional Italian haute cuisine.

2 MICHELIN Stars

Il Pagliaccio

Chef Anthony Genovese's intimate restaurant blending Italian and Asian influences with creative tasting menus and impeccable wine pairings.

Traditional Roman

Da Enzo al 29

Authentic Trastevere trattoria serving classic Roman dishes like cacio e pepe, carbonara, and amatriciana. No reservations—arrive early for this local favorite.

Deli & Restaurant

Roscioli

A legendary family-run deli-restaurant famous for its cured meats, artisanal cheeses, and perfectly executed Roman classics with an extensive wine selection.

Modern Italian

Antico Arco

Contemporary Roman cuisine on Gianicolo Hill offering innovative dishes with seasonal ingredients and stunning sunset views over the city.

Seafood Excellence

Pierluigi

Historic fish restaurant near Campo de' Fiori serving the freshest seafood with impeccable service in an elegant piazza setting since 1938.

1 MICHELIN Star

Imàgo

Located atop Hotel Hassler with panoramic terrace views. Chef Andrea Antonini creates sophisticated Mediterranean cuisine with theatrical presentation.

Family Tradition

Armando al Pantheon

Third-generation family trattoria steps from the Pantheon. Celebrated for traditional Roman recipes passed down through generations since 1961.

Best Pizza al Taglio

Pizzarium Bonci

Legendary pizza by the slice from master baker Gabriele Bonci. Creative toppings on perfectly fermented dough—a must-visit near the Vatican.
