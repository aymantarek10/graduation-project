Must-Visit Attractions

Sacred Mountain

Mount Fuji

Japan's iconic symbol standing at 3,776 meters. Climb the volcanic peak during summer season or view its stunning silhouette from Lake Kawaguchi across all seasons.

Thousand Torii

Fushimi Inari Shrine

Kyoto's most iconic shrine with thousands of vermillion torii gates creating mesmerizing tunnels. Wander through sacred pathways surrounded by temples and nature.

Urban Energy

Shibuya Crossing

World's busiest pedestrian crossing where thousands converge simultaneously. Experience Tokyo's vibrant energy, neon lights, and modern culture at this iconic intersection.

Bamboo Forest

Arashiyama Bamboo Grove

Kyoto's most visited natural attraction featuring towering bamboo stalks creating a serene green tunnel. Walk through this magical forest in peaceful tranquility.

Golden Temple

Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion)

Kyoto's most photographed temple with stunning gold-leaf-covered architecture reflected in Mirror Pond. A UNESCO World Heritage site of breathtaking beauty.

Tokyo Landmark

Tokyo Tower

Iconic red tower standing 333 meters tall with observation decks offering panoramic Tokyo views. Experience the city's electric energy from above, especially stunning at night.

Culinary Excellence

3 MICHELIN Stars

Sukiyabashi Jiro

Tokyo's legendary sushi restaurant operated by master chef Jiro Ono. Omakase experience featuring pristine seasonal fish and perfect rice crafted with 50+ years of expertise.

Ramen House

Ippudo Ramen

Japan's most popular ramen chain with tonkotsu (pork bone) broth specialty. Experience authentic Japanese comfort food with perfect noodle texture and rich, flavorful broth.

Traditional Tempura

Tempura Kondo

Tokyo's finest tempura restaurant featuring seasonal vegetables and seafood fried to perfection. Counter seating allows you to watch master chefs prepare each piece.

Kaiseki Dining

Kikunoi

Kyoto's most prestigious kaiseki restaurant operating since 1912. Multi-course haute cuisine showcasing seasonal ingredients and traditional Japanese culinary artistry.

Savory Pancake

Okonomiyaki Kiji

Osaka's beloved okonomiyaki (savory pancake) restaurant established in 1945. Watch chefs flip layered pancakes filled with cabbage, meat, and special sauce at traditional teppan counter.

Pork Cutlet

Tonki Tonkatsu

Tokyo's legendary tonkatsu restaurant serving perfectly breaded and fried pork cutlets. Pair with homemade tonkatsu sauce, fresh cabbage, and steaming rice.
