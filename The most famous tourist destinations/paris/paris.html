Iconic Landmarks

Paris Icon

Eiffel Tower

The world's most recognizable landmark standing 330 meters tall. Welcomes 7 million visitors annually. Enjoy breathtaking views from three observation decks and watch it sparkle every hour after sunset.

World's Largest Museum

The Louvre Museum

Home to the Mona Lisa and over 38,000 artworks. This former royal palace showcases masterpieces from ancient civilizations to the 19th century. The iconic glass pyramid is a modern architectural marvel.

Most Beautiful Avenue

Champs-Élysées

Stretching 1.9 kilometers from Place de la Concorde to the Arc de Triomphe. Features luxury boutiques, authentic Parisian cafés, and theaters. Perfect for shopping and soaking in Parisian elegance.

Historic Monument

Arc de Triomphe

Built by Napoleon Bonaparte to honor the French army. Standing 50 meters tall at Place Charles de Gaulle. Climb to the top for panoramic views of 12 radiating avenues.

Gothic Masterpiece

Notre-Dame Cathedral

A 12th-century Gothic architectural masterpiece. Famous for its stained glass windows and carved sculptures. Currently under restoration after the 2019 fire, expected to reopen December 2025.

Montmartre Summit

Sacré-Cœur Basilica

A stunning white basilica perched atop Montmartre hill, the highest point in Paris. Enjoy spectacular city views and explore the charming artistic neighborhood surrounding it.

Royal Palace

Palace of Versailles

The magnificent royal palace that housed French kings. Famous for the legendary Hall of Mirrors and stunning gardens spanning 800 hectares. An unforgettable day trip 20 km from Paris.

Impressionist Art

Musée d'Orsay

Houses the world's largest collection of Impressionist paintings. A former train station transformed into a museum showcasing works by Monet, Renoir, Van Gogh, and other art giants.

River Cruise

Seine River

Discover Paris from a different angle with a Seine River cruise. Pass under historic bridges and see the city's major landmarks from the water, especially magical in the evening with twinkling lights.

Luxury Hotels

Parisian Legend

Ritz Paris

An icon of luxury in Place Vendôme since 1898. The legendary hotel that has hosted celebrities and royalty. Enjoy classic grandeur, the exclusive Chanel spa, and the famous Hemingway Bar.

Prince's Palace

Shangri-La Paris

Napoleon's former palace with direct views of the Eiffel Tower. Combines historical grandeur with refined Asian service. Features L'Abeille restaurant with two Michelin stars.

3 Michelin Restaurants

Four Seasons George V

Steps from the Champs-Élysées. The only hotel in Europe with three Michelin-starred restaurants. Classic French décor and unparalleled service.

Royal Elegance

Le Meurice

Authentic Parisian hotel overlooking the Tuileries Garden. Louis XVI-style décor, Michelin-starred Le Meurice Alain Ducasse restaurant, and a perfect location in the heart of Paris.

Parisian Chic

Plaza Athénée

Symbol of Parisian elegance on the famous Avenue Montaigne. Eiffel Tower views, Alain Ducasse's 3-Michelin-star restaurant, and the exclusive Dior Institute spa.

Contemporary Haven

Mandarin Oriental Paris

Sleek contemporary design in the heart of Rue Saint-Honoré. Blends Parisian luxury with Asian elegance. Features a charming courtyard garden and two-Michelin-star Sur Mesure restaurant.

World-Class Dining

3 MICHELIN Stars

Alain Ducasse au Plaza Athénée

An exceptional dining experience from legendary Chef Alain Ducasse. A menu focused on fish, vegetables, and grains prepared innovatively in luxurious surroundings.

3 MICHELIN Stars

Le Cinq

At Four Seasons George V. Chef Christian Le Squer presents classic French cuisine with a modern touch in an opulent Louis XVI-style dining room.

3 MICHELIN Stars

L'Oiseau Blanc

On the rooftop of The Peninsula Paris with stunning Eiffel Tower views. Refined French cuisine in an atmosphere evoking the golden age of aviation.

Unique Experience

Septime

A contemporary Michelin-starred restaurant. Chef Bertrand Grébaut presents innovative dishes based on seasonal ingredients in an informal, intimate atmosphere.

In the Eiffel Tower

Le Jules Verne

Michelin-starred restaurant on the second floor of the Eiffel Tower. Enjoy refined French cuisine with incredible Paris views at 125 meters high.

Authentic Brasserie

Brasserie Lipp

A historic Parisian institution in Saint-Germain-des-Prés since 1880. Authentic French brasserie experience with Art Nouveau décor and classic dishes like choucroute.

Elevated Daily Fare

Le Comptoir du Relais

From Chef Yves Camdeborde in the heart of Saint-Germain. Authentic French bistro cuisine with fresh ingredients and traditional preparation. Warm and intimate atmosphere.

Historic Café

Café de Flore

One of the most famous Parisian cafés since 1887. Meeting place for intellectuals and artists like Sartre and Hemingway. Enjoy French coffee and croissants in legendary surroundings.

Famous Hot Chocolate

Angelina

Legendary tearoom near the Louvre. Famous for its rich hot chocolate and Mont Blanc dessert. A classic Parisian experience in elegant Belle Époque décor.
