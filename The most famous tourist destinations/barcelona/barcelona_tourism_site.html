Iconic Luxury Hotels

5-Star Beachfront

Hotel Arts Barcelona

A striking 44-story tower on the beachfront designed by SOM architects. Features two Michelin-starred restaurants, rooftop pools, and stunning Mediterranean views.

Passeig de Gràcia

Mandarin Oriental, Barcelona

Located on Barcelona's most fashionable boulevard, this elegant hotel combines contemporary luxury with Catalan style, featuring a rooftop pool and exceptional spa.

Iconic Design

W Barcelona

The sail-shaped 'Hotel Vela' on Barceloneta Beach offers cutting-edge design, vibrant nightlife at Eclipse Bar, and breathtaking sea and city views.

Classic Elegance

Majestic Hotel & Spa Barcelona

A century-old landmark on Passeig de Gràcia with neoclassical architecture, rooftop pool, and proximity to Casa Batlló and La Pedrera.

Historic Luxury

El Palace Barcelona

Since 1919, this palace hotel has hosted royalty and celebrities. Features stunning belle époque architecture, Michelin-starred dining, and impeccable service.

Boutique Charm

Cotton House Hotel

A beautifully restored 19th-century textile headquarters blending neoclassical grandeur with contemporary design in the Gothic Quarter.

Must-Visit Attractions

UNESCO World Heritage

Sagrada Família

Gaudí's masterpiece basilica under construction since 1882. This architectural marvel attracts over 4.5 million visitors annually with its intricate facades and stunning interior light.

Gaudí's Garden

Park Güell

A whimsical public park designed by Gaudí featuring colorful mosaics, organic architecture, and panoramic views of Barcelona from Carmel Hill.

Iconic Boulevard

Las Ramblas

Barcelona's most famous tree-lined pedestrian street stretching 1.2 km from Plaça de Catalunya to the Port Vell. Experience street performers, markets, and vibrant atmosphere.

Football Temple

Camp Nou Stadium

Europe's largest football stadium and home to FC Barcelona. Currently undergoing renovation to expand capacity to 105,000 seats with completion expected in 2026.

Mediterranean Coast

Barceloneta Beach

Barcelona's most famous urban beach stretching 1.1 km along the Mediterranean. Enjoy golden sand, beachfront restaurants, water sports, and stunning city skyline views.

Gaudí Masterpiece

Casa Batlló

A stunning example of Gaudí's architectural genius on Passeig de Gràcia. The building's facade resembles a dragon's back with colorful mosaics and organic forms.

Medieval Heart

Gothic Quarter (Barri Gòtic)

Barcelona's oldest neighborhood with narrow medieval streets, Roman ruins, Gothic cathedral, and hidden plazas. A labyrinth of history dating back 2,000 years.

Food Market

La Boqueria Market

One of Europe's most famous food markets on Las Ramblas. Experience vibrant colors, fresh produce, local specialties, and authentic Catalan cuisine since 1840.

Olympic Hill

Montjuïc

Historic hilltop offering gardens, museums, Olympic stadiums from 1992, Montjuïc Castle, and the magical Magic Fountain light and music show.

World-Class Dining

3 MICHELIN Stars

Lasarte

Chef Martín Berasategui's Barcelona flagship with three Michelin stars. Experience refined Basque-Mediterranean cuisine in an elegant setting at Monument Hotel.

2 MICHELIN Stars

Disfrutar

Three El Bulli alumni create avant-garde Mediterranean cuisine with playful presentations. Named among World's 50 Best Restaurants with innovative tasting menus.

1 MICHELIN Star

Cinc Sentits

Contemporary Catalan cuisine focusing on seasonal local ingredients. Chef Jordi Artal creates sophisticated dishes that engage all five senses.

Tapas Excellence

Cervecería Catalana

Barcelona's favorite tapas bar on Passeig de Gràcia. Always packed with locals enjoying traditional Spanish tapas, fresh seafood, and excellent vermouth.

Ferran Adrià Creation

Tickets

Albert Adrià's innovative tapas bar offering creative small plates in a circus-themed setting. Molecular gastronomy meets traditional Spanish flavors.

Since 1786

Can Culleretes

Barcelona's oldest restaurant serving authentic Catalan cuisine for over 235 years. Experience traditional dishes in a historic Gothic Quarter setting.

2 MICHELIN Stars

Moments

Chef Carme Ruscalleda and son Raül Balam's restaurant at Mandarin Oriental. Contemporary Catalan haute cuisine with impeccable technique and presentation.

Seafood Legend

Cal Pep

Legendary seafood bar in El Born serving the freshest fish and shellfish. No reservations—arrive early for counter seating and Chef Pep's daily specials.

1 MICHELIN Star

Enigma

Albert Adrià's gastronomic experience with 40+ courses through multiple spaces. A theatrical journey blending art, science, and culinary innovation.
