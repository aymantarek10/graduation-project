Unforgettable Attractions

The Red City

Marrakech

The magical city with legendary palaces and the famous Jemaa el-Fnaa square. Explore colorful spice souks, stunning Majorelle Gardens, and the historic Bahia Palace.

Golden Sand Dunes

Merzouga Desert

Morocco's highest sand dunes reaching 150 meters! Experience camel trekking at sunset and sleep under star-filled skies in authentic Berber camps.

The Blue Pearl

Chefchaouen

Jewel of the Rif Mountains with enchanting blue-washed streets. Every corner is a natural art canvas! Perfect for walking, photography, and peaceful relaxation.

Cultural Capital

Fes

The oldest imperial city and UNESCO World Heritage Site. Visit traditional leather tanneries, Al Quaraouiyine University (world's oldest), and stunning ancient souks.

Economic Capital

Casablanca

The magnificent Hassan II Mosque with its soaring 200m minaret overlooking the Atlantic Ocean. A modern blend of traditional architecture and contemporary design.

Hollywood Film Location

Ait Benhaddou

Stunning earthen clay kasbah and UNESCO World Heritage Site. Featured in Game of Thrones and Gladiator. Breathtaking traditional Berber architecture!

City of Art

Asilah

Charming coastal town on the Atlantic Ocean. Its walls transform into an open-air art gallery every summer, with peaceful beaches and old Portuguese ramparts.

Snow-Capped Peaks

Atlas Mountains

Majestic mountain range with Mount Toubkal (4,167m) - North Africa's highest peak. Amazing trekking experience with authentic Berber villages nestled in the mountains.

Atlantic Gem

Essaouira

Charming coastal city with ancient ramparts and vibrant harbor. Perfect for windsurfing and exploring the historic fortified medina with its Portuguese influence.

Legendary Luxury Hotels

Historic Palace

La Mamounia, Marrakech

Legendary palace hotel since 1923, hosted Winston Churchill and Alfred Hitchcock. Stunning 20-acre gardens, world-class spa, and royal 5-star service.

Ultimate Luxury

Royal Mansour, Marrakech

Architectural masterpiece built by King Mohammed VI. Each suite is a private 3-story riad with 24/7 butler service and secret underground tunnels for staff.

Desert Resort

Four Seasons Safari Lodge Marrakech

African safari experience in the heart of Morocco! Luxurious lodges with Atlas Mountain views and a private wildlife reserve with exotic animals.

Ocean Views

Ritz-Carlton Rabat

Luxury hotel on the Atlantic Ocean shore in the capital. Combines contemporary elegance with authentic Moroccan hospitality, featuring spa and golf course.

Art Deco Icon

Sofitel Casablanca Tour Blanche

Elegant skyscraper in the heart of Casablanca. Panoramic 360-degree views of the city and ocean from Morocco's tallest tower hotel.

Luxury Desert Camp

Kasbah Bab Ourika, Atlas

Charming mountain resort on the banks of Ourika River. Elegantly designed rooms and suites with breathtaking views of the High Atlas Mountains.

Asian Opulence

Mandarin Oriental, Marrakech

Luxurious oasis on 20 hectares of gardens. Private villas with pools, award-winning spa, and world-class restaurants serving international cuisine.

Boutique Riad

Riad Tarabel, Marrakech

Charming traditional riad with only 5 rooms. Authentic Moroccan courtyard with fountain, ornate ceilings, and intimate personalized service.

Beach Resort

Mazagan Beach & Golf Resort

Luxury beachfront resort on 250 hectares. Casino, championship golf course, massive spa, and private beach on the Atlantic Ocean.

Exceptional Dining Experiences

Best in Marrakech

Dar Yacout

Traditional palace with magical ambiance and old medina views. Authentic Moroccan tagine and luxury seafood appetizers in Arabian Nights atmosphere.

Fine Dining

Le Marocain, La Mamounia

Award-winning restaurant in La Mamounia palace. Moroccan cuisine with modern luxury touches, live musical performances, and royal atmosphere.

French-Moroccan

La Cantine du Troquet

Contemporary French restaurant in the heart of Casablanca. Classic French dishes with Moroccan touches and an excellent wine selection.

Authentic Experience

Restaurant Al Fassia

All-female restaurant serving authentic traditional Moroccan cuisine. Famous for tagine and couscous prepared with secret family recipes!

Rooftop Dining

Le Foundouk, Marrakech

Stylish restaurant in a restored caravanserai. Mediterranean-Moroccan fusion cuisine with spectacular rooftop terrace overlooking the medina.

Movie Legend

Rick's Caf, Casablanca

Recreated from the classic film "Casablanca." Piano bar, 1940s ambiance, and international menu with Moroccan specialties in an iconic setting.

Farm to Table

La Table du March

Fresh market cuisine by Chef Christophe Leroy. Daily changing menu based on seasonal ingredients from local markets and farms.

Modern Moroccan

Nomad, Marrakech

Contemporary take on Moroccan classics with stunning medina views. Three-story restaurant with rooftop terrace and innovative fusion dishes.

Italian Excellence

Pepe Nero, Marrakech

Authentic Italian restaurant in a beautiful riad setting. Homemade pasta, wood-fired pizzas, and imported Italian ingredients in romantic garden atmosphere.
