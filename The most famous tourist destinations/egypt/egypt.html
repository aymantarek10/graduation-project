Must-Visit Attractions

Seven Wonders

Pyramids of Giza & Sphinx

Among the oldest and greatest monuments in the world, built over 4,500 years ago. The three pyramids attract millions of visitors annually to witness this ancient engineering marvel.

World's Largest

Grand Egyptian Museum

The world's largest archaeological museum dedicated to a single civilization, housing over 100,000 artifacts including the complete Tutankhamun treasures displayed together for the first time.

Largest Ancient Temple

Karnak Temple Complex

The world's largest temple complex spanning 200 acres. The Great Hypostyle Hall features 134 massive columns reaching 21 meters high, creating an awe-inspiring forest of stone.

Engineering Marvel

Abu Simbel Temples

Ramses II's rock-cut temples, completely relocated in the 1960s to save them from flooding. Twice annually, sunlight illuminates the inner sanctuary in a spectacular solar alignment.

Royal Tombs

Valley of the Kings

Contains 63 tombs of ancient Egyptian pharaohs including the famous tomb of Tutankhamun. The carvings and colors remain remarkably preserved after thousands of years.

Islamic Heritage

Citadel of Saladin

A fortified citadel built in 1176 CE atop Mokattam Hill. Houses the famous Muhammad Ali Mosque, military museums, and offers panoramic views of Cairo.

World-Class Diving

Sharm El Sheikh & Hurghada

Among the world's best diving destinations with stunning coral reefs. Crystal-clear waters year-round and exceptional marine biodiversity in the Red Sea.

Desert Wonder

White Desert & Oases

Unique white rock formations resembling sculptures. Experience camping under one of the world's clearest skies and visit lush green oases in the desert.

Island of Gods

Philae Temple, Aswan

Temple of goddess Isis on Philae Island, relocated stone by stone to its current location. Evening sound and light shows create an enchanting experience.

Iconic Luxury Hotels

Nile View

Four Seasons Nile Plaza

Located in the heart of Cairo on the banks of the Nile. Offers stunning views of the Nile and pyramids, with elegant service and award-winning international restaurants.

Historic Palace

Marriott Mena House

A former royal palace located steps from the Pyramids. Rich history spanning over 150 years, having hosted kings and presidents from around the world.

Colonial Elegance

Sofitel Legend Old Cataract Aswan

Legendary hotel opened in 1899, hosted Agatha Christie who wrote her famous novel here. Enchanting views of the Nile and Elephantine Island.

Beach Resort

Four Seasons Sharm El Sheikh

Luxury resort on the Red Sea with private beaches and yacht marina. Perfect for diving and relaxation with views of the Strait of Tiran and Sanafir Island.

Modern Design

Kempinski Nile Hotel Cairo

Luxury skyscraper in the heart of Cairo on the Nile. Combines European elegance with Egyptian hospitality and world-class facilities.

Heart of Luxor

Steigenberger Nile Palace Luxor

Located directly next to Luxor Temple on the Nile Corniche. Exceptional location allows walking to the most important pharaonic temples with panoramic views.

Complete Resort

Ritz-Carlton El Gouna

Luxury resort in El Gouna tourist city on the Red Sea. Unique architectural design inspired by Nubian architecture with comprehensive sports and water facilities.

New Opening 2025

Waldorf Astoria Cairo

Opened in 2025 in a 250-meter tower in the new Central Business District. Offers a modern luxury experience with views of Greater Cairo.

Indian Luxury

Oberoi Sahl Hasheesh, Hurghada

Luxury resort combining famous Indian hospitality with Red Sea beauty. Extended private beach and spa facilities among the best resorts in the region.

World-Class Dining

Best in Africa

Zooba

Named Best Restaurant in Africa and the Middle East. Serves authentic Egyptian cuisine with a modern twist in elegant atmosphere reflecting Egyptian heritage.

Nile View

Sequoia

Upscale restaurant directly on the Nile with enchanting outdoor terrace. Serves Mediterranean and international cuisine in sophisticated and contemporary atmosphere.

Authentic Egyptian

Abou El Sid Restaurant

The most famous traditional Egyptian restaurant in the heart of Cairo. Dcor reflects ancient Egyptian architecture with authentic dishes prepared using family recipes.

Luxury Japanese

Nobu Cairo

Branch of the world-famous Japanese restaurant chain. Serves innovative Japanese-Peruvian dishes at Four Seasons Nile Plaza with spectacular views.

Seafood

Byblos, Alexandria

Famous fish and seafood restaurant on Alexandria Corniche. Fresh fish daily from the Mediterranean Sea with enchanting sea views.

Elegant Lebanese

Sachi

Upscale Lebanese restaurant in the heart of Zamalek. Serves the finest authentic Lebanese cuisine in elegant atmosphere with exceptional service.

International Variety

Cairo Kitchen

At Kempinski Nile, offers a luxury international buffet and live cooking stations. Interactive culinary experience with a wide selection of global cuisines.

Fine Italian

Ossigeno

Elegant Italian restaurant in Soho Square, Sharm El Sheikh. Serves authentic Italian cuisine with imported ingredients and romantic seaside atmosphere.

Pyramids View

Restaurant 1902

At Marriott Mena House, serves Mediterranean cuisine with direct views of the Pyramids. An unforgettable dining experience in an exceptional historic location.
