Must-Visit Attractions

Great Migration

Maasai Mara National Reserve

Witness the spectacular Great Migration with over 2 million wildebeest crossing the Mara River. Home to the Big Five and some of Africa's most iconic wildlife encounters.

Elephant Paradise

Amboseli National Park

Famous for large elephant herds with breathtaking views of Mount Kilimanjaro. Perfect for wildlife photography with dramatic African landscapes as backdrop.

Tropical Paradise

Diani Beach

Pristine white sandy beaches with turquoise waters and swaying palm trees. Voted Africa's leading beach destination, perfect for diving, kite surfing, and relaxation.

Pink Flamingos

Lake Nakuru National Park

Famous for millions of pink flamingos creating a stunning natural spectacle. Also home to both black and white rhinos, making it one of Kenya's best rhino sanctuaries.

Africa's Second Highest

Mount Kenya National Park

UNESCO World Heritage Site with snow-capped peaks at the equator. Offers challenging trekking routes through diverse ecosystems from bamboo forests to alpine zones.

Swahili Heritage

Lamu Old Town

Kenya's oldest living town and UNESCO World Heritage Site. Experience authentic Swahili culture, narrow winding streets, traditional dhow sailing, and centuries-old architecture.

Largest Park

Tsavo National Parks

One of the world's largest game reserves, famous for red elephants colored by red volcanic soil. Diverse landscapes including rivers, waterfalls, and volcanic hills.

Adventure Park

Hell's Gate National Park

Unique park where you can walk, cycle, or rock climb among wildlife. Dramatic scenery with towering cliffs, volcanic rocks, and geothermal hot springs.

Geological Wonder

Great Rift Valley

Part of the massive geological fault system stretching through Africa. Stunning viewpoints, crater lakes, and diverse ecosystems from the Rift Valley escarpment.

Iconic Luxury Hotels

Instagram Famous

Giraffe Manor, Nairobi

Iconic boutique hotel where endangered Rothschild giraffes visit for breakfast. Built in 1932, this elegant manor offers an unforgettable wildlife experience in the city.

Luxury Safari

Angama Mara

Perched high above the Great Rift Valley with spectacular views over the Maasai Mara. Each tent features floor-to-ceiling glass for uninterrupted wildlife viewing.

Historic Elegance

Fairmont Mount Kenya Safari Club

Former private retreat of Hollywood celebrities, founded by William Holden in 1959. Set on 100 acres with stunning Mount Kenya views and an animal orphanage.

Coastal Luxury

Hemingways Watamu

Boutique beachfront hotel on Kenya's pristine coastline. Offers world-class deep-sea fishing, diving in marine parks, and exclusive access to secluded beaches.

Colonial Heritage

Sarova Stanley, Nairobi

Historic hotel since 1902 in the heart of Nairobi. The famous Thorn Tree Café has been a meeting point for explorers, writers, and adventurers for over a century.

Celebrity Safari

Mahali Mzuri, Maasai Mara

Sir Richard Branson's luxury tented camp in the Maasai Mara. Sustainable eco-camp with 12 luxurious tents offering front-row seats to the Great Migration.

Private Villas

Alfajiri Villas, Diani Beach

Three exclusive cliff-top villas with infinity pools overlooking the Indian Ocean. Ultra-luxury with private chefs, butlers, and direct beach access.

Luxury Tented Camp

Finch Hattons, Tsavo West

Named after Denys Finch Hatton from "Out of Africa." Elegant tented suites on the edge of natural springs with resident hippos and abundant wildlife.

Beach Paradise

The Sands at Nomad, Diani

Boutique beach resort with only 40 cottages nestled in tropical gardens. Direct access to pristine beach with exceptional diving and water sports facilities.

World-Class Dining

Nairobi's Best

Talisman Restaurant

Award-winning restaurant in Karen with a beautiful garden setting. Serves fusion cuisine blending African, Asian, and European flavors with fresh local ingredients.

Iconic Experience

Carnivore Restaurant

World-famous all-you-can-eat meat experience. Serves exotic game meats on Maasai swords, roasted on a huge charcoal pit. A Kenyan institution since 1980.

Italian Fine Dining

La Pergola

Elegant Italian restaurant at Villa Rosa Kempinski. Authentic Italian cuisine with imported ingredients, extensive wine cellar, and sophisticated atmosphere.

Coastal Seafood

Tamarind, Mombasa

Premier seafood restaurant on the waterfront with stunning ocean views. Famous for fresh catches, traditional dhow dinner cruises, and Swahili-inspired dishes.

Unique Setting

Ali Barbour's Cave Restaurant

Incredible dining experience in a 180,000-year-old coral cave. Romantic candlelit atmosphere with seafood specialties and views of stars through natural openings.

Authentic Kenyan

Mama Oliech Restaurant

Famous for authentic Kenyan fish dishes, especially tilapia and Nile perch. A local favorite serving traditional cuisine in a casual, welcoming atmosphere.

Brazilian Steakhouse

Fogo Gaucho

Authentic Brazilian churrascaria with endless servings of premium meats. Rodízio-style dining with salad bar and traditional Brazilian sides in elegant setting.

Mediterranean Fusion

Mediterráneo Restaurant

Rooftop dining at Tribe Hotel with spectacular city views. Mediterranean-inspired menu with emphasis on fresh ingredients and creative presentation.

Indian Cuisine

Haandi Restaurant

Iconic Indian restaurant in Nairobi's Westlands. Serves authentic North Indian cuisine with tandoori specialties, rich curries, and traditional flavors.
